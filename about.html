Who we are?

We are a delivery company that strives to make getting grocery easier and more accesible. We work to deliver an essential service that customers rely on to get their groceries, while also offering safe and flexible earnings opportunies to individuals that sign up to be a Grocery Vancouver's shoppers. Our purpose is to connect you with a wide range of shoppers in your area who can pick up and deliver your groceries to your front door.

We know how important is it for you to be able to shop for groceries at your favourite stores. We work hard to get you the stores you want and the exact sale prices in store on our site.

COVID-19 News from CTV News

Never have Canadians thought more about securing their groceries than they are right now. They are lining up to get inside stores, spending more money on food to prepare at home and expressing a new appreciation for the essential food chain keeping the country fed during this pandemic.

Virtually overnight, hitting the grocery store went from a routine outing to a surreal, experience requiring extensive pre-planning and hyper-vigilance.

What can we do to help?

Use alcohol-based hand sanitizer or wash your hands with soap and water. If you use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer, make sure you use and store it carefully. Keep alcohol-based hand sanitizers out of children's reach. Teach them how to apply the sanitizer and monitor its use. Apply a coin-sized amount on your hands. There is no need to use a large amount of the product.
